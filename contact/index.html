Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship worldwide! Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. Contact us for specific shipping quotes.

How long does it take to make a custom order?

Custom orders typically take 3-4 weeks, depending on the complexity of the design and embroidery work required.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for unused items in original packaging. Custom orders are non-returnable.

Are your products really handmade?

Yes! Every piece is 100% handcrafted by skilled artisans. Each garment comes with a certificate of authenticity.

Do you offer bulk/wholesale orders?

Yes, we work with boutiques and retailers. Please contact us for wholesale pricing and minimum order quantities.
